## Meaning

A Gujarati Bhakti Stavan in which each verse builds toward a single wish, held in the refrain: *"Lord, I ask only this — that my breath leave me while I am in devotion of you, and that your meditation stay in the lotus of my heart."* Verse by verse: may I keep gazing at your gentle face, singing your praise day and night, so that your name stays with me at the hour of death; do not turn my hope into despair, do not hold my faults against me, let every breath I draw be thought of you; absorb my sin and heat, keep this servant at your feet, come and grant me darshan as alms; I have lived on the hope of you, I have pined to meet you, keep me by your side; remove the sins of lifetimes, take my plea into your heart, and stay with me through every birth. At the besnu, the bhajan is a parting benediction to Lila Baa — the wish she herself would have made — and a lesson for the living on how to arrange death.
