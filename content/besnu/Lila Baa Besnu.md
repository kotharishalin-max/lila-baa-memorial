# Lila Baa Besnu

A memorial gathering (*besnu*) for **Shrimati Lilavatiben Champaklal Kothari** — known in the family as **Ba** to her children and grandchildren, and **Liba** to her great-grandchildren — held on **April 20, 2026 at Sindhu Bhawan Hall, Ahmedabad**, four days after she reached Shriji's feet on **April 16, 2026**, at **97 years and 10 months**.

Lila Baa left behind four children, eleven grandchildren, and nineteen great-grandchildren. She spent roughly half her life in India and half in America — most often in the Barai household — and remained fiercely independent to the end, gardening dudhi, brinjal, and tomatoes in the backyard at 97, planning a solo flight back to America in the week before her passing. Her Emirates flight via Dubai was cancelled when the Dubai airport was hit during the war situation; a rescheduled April 29 trip never happened.

## Recurring themes

- **Body as temporary vessel** — *Pankhida Ne Aa Pinjaru* (03), *Junu To Thayu Re Deval* (08), *Raakhna Ramakada* (09) — all use variations of the same image: the body is an old cage or temple the soul has outgrown.
- **Surrender to Shrinathji** (Pushtimarg tradition) — *Ami Bhareli Najru Rakho* (06), *Mara Ghat Ma Birajta Shrinathji* (17), *Saghlu Tane Sompi Didhu* (19). The family's spiritual vocabulary at the besnu ("Shriji charan pamya che" — *reached Shriji's feet*) belongs to this tradition.
- **A mother's incomparable love** — *Janani Ni Jod Sakhi* (11) and *Bhulo Bhale Biju Badhu* (24, Shalin's tribute), both honoring Ba as a mother and grandmother.
- **The journey being God's to determine** — *Hari Tu Gadu Maru* (18) and *Saghlu Tane Sompi Didhu* (19).
- **Universal prayer-meeting anthems** — *Itni Shakti* (04), *O Paalanhaare* (14), *Tu Pyar Ka Sagar Hai* (21).
