# Eulogy by Bharatbhai Barai — Son-in-law of Lila Baa

Part of [[Lila Baa Besnu]] — track 13 of 25.

## Summary (English)

The recording begins with a female emcee welcoming attendees to Lilavatiben Champaklal Kothari's besnu and naming each of her surviving children and their spouses: son Dr. Kiranbhai Kothari and daughter-in-law Sandhyaben; daughter Dr. Pannaben Barai and son-in-law Dr. Bharatbhai Barai; daughter Niharikaben Parikh and son-in-law Anilbhai Parikh; daughter Binaben Shah and son-in-law Rajitbhai Shah. She then invites Dr. Bharatbhai Barai to speak. Dr. Barai reflects on his deep bond with his mother-in-law since his 1970 engagement to Panna, noting she always treated him as a son. After moving to America in 1975, his family hosted Lilaba almost every year. He shares how she helped raise their eldest daughter Sujata (now a Harvard Law School graduate and successful lawyer) during his and Panna's residency, and how she cared for all their children and grandchildren in turn — becoming "home" for the American family. He recounts how the young generation loved her fiercely, quoting them saying "Lilaba cannot be sick, we all need her," and describes how, at 97, she still grew dudhi, brinjal, and tomatoes in the backyard daily. Her planned March flight to America was cancelled when bombs struck Dubai airport and her Emirates booking was cut 12 hours before departure; a rescheduled April 29 trip never happened after a fall led to her final decline. He closes with "Jai Shri Krishna" and the hope that her blessings will continue from heaven.

એ જ રીતે અમારા બધા બાળકો જ્યારે જન્મ્યા ત્યારે બા હંમેશા હાજર હતા. બધાની કાળજી રાખી. અને પછી જ્યારે અમારા grand children જન્મ્યા તે વખતે પણ બા દરેક વખતે America માં જ હતા. એ બધાનું ધ્યાન રાખવું, એમને ખવડાવું પીવડાવું. અને આ બધા બાળકો સૌથી વધારે વ્યથિત થયા છે. એમને બા માટે ઘણો પ્રેમ હતો. જ્યારે અમે કહ્યું કે બા hospital માં છે, ત્યારે કહે, Lilaba cannot be sick, we all need her. અને જ્યારે જ્યારે એમને ઘરમાં કંઈ વાંકું પડે ને બા સાથે ફરિયાદ કરવી હોય, અને જો બા અહીંયા હોય તો face time કરીને ફરિયાદ કરે કે જો બા, અમને આવું નથી થતું.
