## Meaning

This much-loved Hindi prayer, written by Abhilash and set to music by Kuldeep Singh for the 1986 film *Ankush*, has outlived its movie to become a standard school and prayer-meeting anthem across India. The singer asks the divine for enough strength that faith never weakens; for a steady walk on the righteous path where no mistake, however inadvertent, is made. Let ignorance be lit by knowledge; let enmity dissolve into friendship; let us think not of what we have received but of what we have offered; let us scatter flowers of happiness and turn every life into a garden. The final couplet is the besnu's wish exactly: *"Pour your river of compassion, and sanctify every corner of every heart."*
