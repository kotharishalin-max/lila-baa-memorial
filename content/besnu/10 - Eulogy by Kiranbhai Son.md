## Summary (English)

The recording captures the opening of Lilavatiben Champaklal Kothari's besnu. An unnamed female emcee opens the gathering by thanking attendees for their support and offering a philosophical reflection on death — describing it not as an end but as a journey of the indestructible soul from darkness toward light and peace. She then invites Dr. Kiranbhai Kothari, the son of the deceased, to address the gathering. Dr. Kiranbhai shares that his mother passed away on April 16, 2026, after living a fulfilling and fiercely independent life of nearly 98 years (97 years, 10 months). He fondly recounts her resilience: she remained healthy enough to plan a solo trip to America just a month before her passing, despite global travel disruptions that forced her Emirates flight via Dubai to be cancelled. He concludes by honoring the legacy of simplicity, endurance, love, and family unity she left behind, and thanks the attendees for their support, closing with "Thank you very much. Om Shanti."
