# Shantakaram Bhujagashayanam — Opening Sanskrit Shlokas

Part of [[Lila Baa Besnu]] — track 01 of 25.

**Audio:** [Track 01 (S3)](s3view://public/lila-baa/audio/01-shantakaram-bhujagashayanam.mp3)

This track is a medley of three classical Sanskrit invocations sung at the opening of the besnu:
1. Vishnu Vandana / Shantakaram Bhujagashayanam
2. Mangalam Bhagwan Vishnu
3. Sarva Mangala Mangalye

## देवनागरी (Devanagari)

**Vishnu Vandana**

शान्ताकारं भुजगशयनं पद्मनाभं सुरेशं
विश्वाधारं गगनसदृशं मेघवर्णं शुभाङ्गम् ।
लक्ष्मीकान्तं कमलनयनं योगिभिर्ध्यानगम्यं
वन्दे विष्णुं भवभयहरं सर्वलोकैकनाथम् ॥

**Mangalam Bhagwan Vishnu**

मङ्गलं भगवान विष्णुः, मङ्गलं गरुडध्वजः ।
मङ्गलं पुण्डरीकाक्षः, मङ्गलाय तनो हरिः ॥

**Sarva Mangala Mangalye**

सर्वमङ्गलमाङ्गल्ये शिवे सर्वार्थसाधिके ।
शरण्ये त्र्यम्बके गौरि नारायणि नमोऽस्तु ते ॥

## Romanized (English script)

**Vishnu Vandana**

Shantakaram Bhujagashayanam Padmanabham Suresham,
Vishvadharam Gaganasadrisham Meghavarnam Shubhangam,
Lakshmikantam Kamalanayanam Yogibhir-dhyana-gamyam,
Vande Vishnum Bhava-bhaya-haram Sarva-lokaika-natham.

**Mangalam Bhagwan Vishnu**

Mangalam Bhagwan Vishnu, Mangalam Garuda-dhwajah,
Mangalam Pundarikaksha, Mangalaya Tano Harih.

**Sarva Mangala Mangalye**

Sarva-mangala-mangalye Shive sarvartha-sadhike,
Sharanye Tryambake Gauri Narayani namo'stu te.

## Meaning

Three classical Sanskrit invocations traditionally sung to open an auspicious gathering. The Vishnu Vandana pictures Lord Vishnu in his serene cosmic form — resting on the serpent Shesha, lotus-navelled, cloud-hued, consort of Lakshmi, lotus-eyed, reachable only through meditation — and bows to him as the remover of worldly fear and the sole protector of all realms. Mangalam Bhagwan Vishnu is a fourfold salutation of auspiciousness to Vishnu under his many forms (Garuda-bannered, lotus-eyed, Hari). Sarva Mangala Mangalye invokes the Goddess in her aspect as the source of all auspiciousness and refuge for all who seek her. Sung at the start of Lila Baa's besnu to consecrate the gathering and invoke peace for her journey.
