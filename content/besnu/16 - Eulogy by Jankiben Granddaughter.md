# Eulogy by Jankiben — Granddaughter of Lila Baa

Part of [[Lila Baa Besnu]] — track 16 of 25.

**Audio:** [Track 16 (S3)](s3view://public/lila-baa/audio/16-eulogy-by-jankiben-granddaughter.mp3)

## Summary (English)

Jankiben, a granddaughter of Lilavatiben, delivers a heartfelt bilingual (Gujarati + English) tribute on behalf of all the grandchildren and great-grandchildren. She remembers her grandmother — affectionately called "Ba" or "Liba" — as the nurturing heart of a family of four children, eleven grandchildren, and nineteen great-grandchildren built over 98 years. Jankiben highlights Ba's quiet gift for caring, recalling how she remembered every family member's favorite food and expressed love through hugs and the gentle Gujarati phrase "beta, tame jamya?" ("child, have you eaten?"). She lists the foods Ba made with her own hands — jalebi, ghughra, mathiya, dahi bhat, limbu sharbat, patra, athanu, kar kar vadi — and notes that some of the family's happiest memories are kitchen and dining-table moments around her. She honors Ba's strength and grace in raising the family after losing Dadaji early, noting that Ba taught them that true strength is found in peace, endurance, and love. Touching family details include how Ba could instantly soothe babies (a moment Sona shared about Kian), and how a great-granddaughter has been named Lila after her. She closes by saying Ba has now become an angel who will be reunited with Dadaji, and ends with "તમે અમારી નજરથી દૂર છો, પણ અમારા દિલથી ક્યારેય દૂર નહીં થાવ" — "You are far from our sight, but never far from our hearts" — "Jai Shri Krishna."
